About Us

Located in Melbourne Australia, The Grey Shed produces boutique hand crafted furniture and decor for customers who value a time gone by when the passion for a craft showed in the final product. Specialising in rustic and artisan appearance, our items are intended for discerning customers who desire the warmth a unique woodwork item can add to their home.

Furniture as unique as you are.

With every item lovingly crafted by hand, combined with the unique nature of wood, you can be sure that the item you select will truly be one of a kind. From decor designed to captivate your guests gaze, to finely crafted jewellery boxes, and pieces that would make the perfect gift for a loved one, we have speciality items to suit every person. So browse our gallery for inspiration and get ready to be captivated by the stunning beauty of hand crafted woodwork.
